The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small hound, similar in apperance to the much larger foxhound. the Beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts,the beagle is the primarily breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. the beagle ts intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper , and a lack of inherited health problems.
